Symplectic Reading Seminar

This weekly online seminar is joint-organized by ShanghaiTech University, USTC-IGP, Yonsei University, and Zhejiang University. This reading seminar aims to study various interesting topics and machinery in symplectic geometry. The topic in this semester (Spring 2023) is the virtual techniques, in particular, the global Kuranishi charts based on the work from Abouzaid-McLean-Smith.

Organizing committee: Hansol Hong, Junwu Tu, Weiwei Wu, Jun Zhang.

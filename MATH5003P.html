MATH5003 （微分流形）， FALL 2024

This is a graduate-level introduction to differential manifolds (mainly on smooth manifolds).

Teaching assistants: 何玟辛，李进钊.

Final Score = 30% Homework + 30% Middle-term Exam + 30% Final Exam.

Classes

September 23, 2024, notes.

Introduction to this course, definition of manifold, Basic examples of manifolds.

-----------------------------

September 24, 2024, notes.

Construct manifolds: product, open subset, quotient, basic examples of Lie groups.

-----------------------------

September 29, 2024, notes.

Group action (by Lie groups), reduction (example: Grassmannian), definition of vector bundle (example: Mobius bundle).

-----------------------------

September 30, 2024, notes.

Construct vector bundles (example: tangent bundle, cotangent bundle), sections (example: vector fields), Poincare-Hopf theorem, directional derivative (of a function on a manifold), definition of connection.

-----------------------------

October 8, 2024, notes.

Connection, Bracket (of vector fields), tensor (of vector spaces), universal property.

-----------------------------

October 14, 2024, notes.

Tensor algebra, tensor bundle, tensor field (example: metric tensors), non-example of tensor fields (connection, bracket), Sym vs. Alt, wedge algebra (definition + basis).

-----------------------------

October 15, 2024, notes.

Wedge product, Hodge star operation, wedge bundle, k-form (as sections of the wedge-k bundle), exterior derivative (formula), dd = 0, interior multiplication.

-----------------------------

October 21, 2024, notes.

Examples in calculs of dd =0 (grad, curl, div), Maxwell's equation (formulation via forms), definition of flowlines, examples of flowline (2-torus sitting in \R^3, constructing symplectic matrices).

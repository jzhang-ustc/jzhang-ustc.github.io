Jun ZHANG（张俊）

I am a tenure-track assistant professor at the Institute of Geometry and Physics at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC-IGP). I am working on SYMPLECTIC TOPOLOGY (what is it?)

Research Interests

My research interests include Hamiltonian dynamics, Floer theory, topological data analysis, and microlocal sheaf theory. More explicitly, I have been studying symplectic and contact geometric objects from various quantitative perspectives and creating new symplectic and contact invariants. My work can be found on arXiv here and my Google Scholar is here.
